Why Choose Doc's Pest Control?

With a passion for helping families and a strong background in pest control, I founded Doc’s Pest Control with a clear mission: to provide effective, safe, and reliable pest solutions tailored to each home’s unique needs. I saw too many companies treating customers like numbers—quick in, quick out, with little care for long-term results or real relationships. I wanted to change that by creating a company rooted in integrity, personal service, and genuine care for the people we serve.

From the start, our focus has been on delivering high-quality service while educating homeowners about prevention and safety. We understand how disruptive pests can be to a household’s comfort and health, and we take pride in offering solutions that are both thorough and family-friendly. Every job we take on is handled with the same attention and commitment we’d give to our own homes.

Today, with over 9 years of hands-on experience, Doc’s Pest Control proudly serves families across the East Valley. We’re grateful to be a trusted part of the community, helping our neighbors protect what matters most. Whether it’s a one-time issue or ongoing prevention, we’re here to ensure your home stays pest-free—so you can enjoy peace of mind, year-round.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your treatments safe for kids and pets?
Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic solutions that are safe for the whole family.
How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
We offer same-day service for most emergency situations in the East Valley area.
Do I need to leave my home during service?
In most cases, no. For standard treatments, you can stay home. We’ll let you know in advance if special conditions apply.
What should I do before a treatment?
We may ask you to move furniture away from walls, remove pet food/water bowls, and secure children’s toys. Full prep instructions will be provided.
How long does a typical treatment take?
Most services take between 30 minutes and 1 hour, depending on the size of the property and type of treatment.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, certified, and insured to perform pest control services in Arizona.
