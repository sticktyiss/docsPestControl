Frequently Asked Questions

Are your treatments safe for kids and pets?
Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic solutions that are safe for the whole family.
How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
We offer same-day service for most emergency situations in the East Valley area.
Do I need to leave my home during service?
In most cases, no. For standard treatments, you can stay home. We’ll let you know in advance if special conditions apply.
What should I do before a treatment?
We may ask you to move furniture away from walls, remove pet food/water bowls, and secure children’s toys. Full prep instructions will be provided.
How long does a typical treatment take?
Most services take between 30 minutes and 1 hour, depending on the size of the property and type of treatment.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, certified, and insured to perform pest control services in Arizona.
